+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
¦ Story                                                                                      ¦
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
    
    You, Star Lotti, find the USS Patchnotes floating in space sending a
	distress signal. You decide to help the USS Patchnotes and enter their
	ship.
